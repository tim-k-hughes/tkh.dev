How I work

Three patterns I come back to again and again.

Design for the handoff

The best operations work usually shows up where ownership gets fuzzy. I like making the transition points between teams feel obvious, documented, and calm.

Build systems people actually use

Process only matters if it survives contact with real work. I care about clarity, adoption, and tools that reduce friction instead of adding ceremony.

Stay close to the details

Strong strategy still needs someone willing to get into dashboards, workflows, reporting, or support threads and make the thing better line by line.
